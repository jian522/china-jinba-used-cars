FAQ

Can I import a used Chinese car to Iraq?

Eligibility depends on current Iraqi import, customs and registration requirements. Confirm the exact model year and specification before payment.

Does the displayed price include shipping to Iraq?

No. Website prices are indicative vehicle prices unless a written quotation explicitly includes freight, insurance, duties or destination charges.

What information is needed for a quotation?

Send the vehicle or stock number, budget, destination city or port, and preferred shipping method if known.
